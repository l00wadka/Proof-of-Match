How to play?

  1. Click on a tile to reveal its image.
  2. Find matching pairs of images.
  3. Scoring:
    1. Matching pair: +25 points (with time bonus)
    2. Wrong pair: -5 points (with time bonus)
    3. Time bonus multiplier: 0.5x to 2.0x
  4. The faster you play, the higher your score multiplier!
  5. Complete the game by finding all pairs.
